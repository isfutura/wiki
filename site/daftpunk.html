Daft Punk

Daft Punk were a French electronic music duo. It was started by Thomas Bangalter and Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo.

Daft Punk made four studio albums in their lifetime: Homework (1997), Discovery (2001), Human After All (2005), and Random Access Memories (2013).

They featured on two songs from The Weeknd's album Starboy (2016): Starboy and I Feel It Coming.

The duo announced their breakup in 2021.
